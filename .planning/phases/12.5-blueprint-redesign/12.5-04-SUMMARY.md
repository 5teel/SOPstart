## Blob Upload Round-Trip Architecture

The plan offered two options for blob upload in VoiceNoteBlock. Wave 4 implements the cleaner split:

1. **Online path:** `VoiceCaptureControl` calls `stop()` which returns `{ blob, transcript, confidence }`. The transcript is passed to `onTranscript`. The blob is NOT uploaded inline — the parent (VoiceNoteBlock) receives the transcript only. Full blob upload + DB insert is deferred to a future signed-URL flow or can be done via the offline queue pattern.
2. **Offline path:** `VoiceCaptureControl.handleStop()` detects `isOnline === false` and queues the blob in `db.voiceNotesQueue`. On reconnect, `flushVoiceNoteQueue` transcribes via REST + uploads to `sop-voice-notes` Storage + calls `saveVoiceNote`.

This means online voice notes only persist transcript text (no blob upload) until the signed-URL upload flow is wired in a future wave. Documented as a known gap.

## iOS Safari / MediaRecorder Format

- `pickRecorderFormat()` runs the PREFERENCE_CHAIN: `audio/webm;codecs=opus` → `audio/webm` → `audio/ogg;codecs=opus` → `audio/mp4`.
- iOS Safari 14.3+ supports `audio/mp4` (AAC). The Deepgram stream uses `encoding: 'aac'` and `sample_rate: '44100'` when mp4 is selected (Pitfall 9 mitigation).
- iOS Safari < 14.3: `isVoiceCaptureSupported()` returns false; VoiceCaptureControl renders graceful degradation message.
