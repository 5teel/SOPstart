PRE-CONDITIONS & ASSUMPTIONS
  • — Line 4 is scheduled for maintenance (see production schedule)

CRITICAL THERMAL
H-01

Residual furnace heat

Lehr exit surface can remain >200°C for 45 min after shutdown. IR burns through thin gloves in <2 s.

CONTROLS
  • · Verify IR reading <80°C before approach (step 2.1)
  • · Wear 260°C-rated gloves within 1 m of furnace body
CRITICAL ELECTRICAL
H-02

Unexpected automatic restart

Watchdog timer may re-energise conveyor if E-stop is released without proper reset sequence. Entrapment risk.

CONTROLS
  • · Lockout-tagout master disconnect before any panel work (step 1.1)
  • · Never release E-stop while personnel are in exclusion zone
HIGH MECHANICAL
H-03

Falling glass sheets

Partially-tempered sheets on conveyor may dislodge when E-stop engages. Edge lacerations are the primary injury.

CONTROLS
  • · Ensure conveyor is clear of sheets before test (step 1.3)
  • · Stand outside 2-m exclusion cone during actuation
HIGH PNEUMATIC
H-04

Stored pneumatic energy

Cooling rollers retain 6 bar air after E-stop. Uncontrolled release during disconnect.

CONTROLS
  • · Bleed pneumatic system via manifold V-07 before inspection
MEDIUM NOISE
H-05

Alarm horn >95 dB

E-stop triggers 95 dB alarm horn at 1 m. Hearing damage on prolonged exposure.

CONTROLS
  • · Wear Class 5 hearing protection if lingering >30 s
LOW VISIBILITY
H-06

Steam plume at quench

Residual quench water creates a ~1 m plume at E-stop event. Visibility reduces for ~10 s.
